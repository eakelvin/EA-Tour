About Us

Welcome to Ghana

Tourism is a key economic driver in Ghana which generates foreign exchange earnings, creates jobs and wealth as well as stimulates other sectors of the economy.

Tourism resources in Ghana are immense and diverse. The country has natural, historical, cultural and other man-made attractions. Its central location on the world map makes it accessible. It is politically and economically stable.

Ghana has over 30 Forts and Castles i.e. Elmina and Cape Coast castles, Manhyia Palace Museum which has historic relics and artifacts of Ashanti Kings. Religious monuments such as Larabanga Mosque which is among the oldest mosques in West Africa.
